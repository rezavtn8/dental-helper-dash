Text Formatting

You can use bold text, italic text, and underlined text. You can also use inline code for technical terms or code snippets.

Section with Checkpoint

This heading has a data-checkpoint attribute, which means progress will be tracked when the user scrolls past it. The border will turn green when completed!

Ordered lists are numbered:

  2. Step two: Then do this

Best Practices

  • Use data-checkpoint on important section headings to track progress
  • Add data-track-id to videos you want to track viewing progress
  • Keep paragraphs concise for better readability
  • Use headings (h1-h6) to create a clear content hierarchy
  • Include images and videos to break up text and illustrate concepts
  • Use blockquotes for important callouts or key takeaways

Conclusion

That's it! Your HTML content will automatically look professional and engaging. Just upload your HTML file to the learning-content storage bucket and the CourseContentViewer will handle the rest.

Pro Tip: The progress bar at the top combines scroll depth, video watching progress, and checkpoint completion to give students a complete picture of their learning journey through this module.
